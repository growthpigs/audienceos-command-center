## Business Context

Urban Thread Co. is a direct-to-consumer sustainable streetwear brand targeting environmentally-conscious millennials and Gen Z consumers. They manufacture using recycled materials and ethical labor practices.

### Current Situation
- Strong brand identity and loyal customer base
- Primary channels: Instagram, TikTok, email marketing
- Running Meta and Google Ads with inconsistent results
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC) trending upward
- Average order value: $85

### Growth Challenges
1. **High ad spend with poor ROAS** - Spending $45K/month on ads with 1.8x ROAS
2. **Creative fatigue** - Same ad formats performing worse over time
3. **Inefficient retargeting** - Missing warm audience opportunities
4. **Attribution chaos** - Can't determine which channels actually convert

## Engagement Scope

### Systems Implemented
1. **Paid Ads Engine**
   - Full Meta Ads restructure (TOF/MOF/BOF)
   - Google Shopping optimization
   - Creative testing framework (DCT methodology)

2. **Retargeting Workflows**
   - Email abandoned cart sequences
   - Dynamic product retargeting
   - Lookalike audience expansion
