## Business Context

GreenLeaf Markets is a regional organic grocery chain focused on local sourcing, sustainable practices, and community engagement. They compete with Whole Foods and local co-ops by emphasizing hyperlocal products and personalized service.

### Current Situation
- Strong brand loyalty in core Portland market
- Expanding to Seattle area (2 new stores planned)
- Mix of in-store and online ordering (20% digital)
- Average basket size: $62
- Customer retention: 78% shop monthly

### Growth Challenges
1. **Manual processes** - Inventory, scheduling, vendor management all spreadsheet-based
2. **Wasted resources** - Staff spending 15+ hours/week on admin tasks
3. **Scaling concerns** - Current systems won't support expansion
4. **Data silos** - No unified view of operations across locations
